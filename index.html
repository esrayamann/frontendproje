Every Child Has Rights

Protecting and promoting the rights of every child for a better future

Fundamental Rights

Right to Life

Every child has the inherent right to life, survival, and development.

Right to Education

All children deserve access to quality education and learning opportunities.

Right to Protection

Children must be protected from violence, abuse, and exploitation.

Education for All

Every child has the right to quality education that helps them develop their talents and abilities. Education should teach children about their rights and prepare them for life.

  • Free primary education
  • Equal access to education
  • Safe learning environment

Protection & Safety

Safe Environment

Children have the right to grow up in a safe and supportive environment.

Health Care

Access to quality healthcare and medical treatment when needed.

Freedom from Abuse

Protection from all forms of violence, abuse, and exploitation.
